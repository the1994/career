There are seven different ways you can return data from two relational tables.

- INNER JOIN
- LEFT JOIN
- RIGHT JOIN
- OUTER JOIN
- LEFT JOIN EXCLUDING INNER JOIN
- RIGHT JOIN EXCLUDING INNER JOIN
- OUTER JOIN EXCLUDING INNER JOIN
